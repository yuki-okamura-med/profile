Yuki T. Okamura

Yuki T. Okamura is a researcher based in Japan and affiliated with the Graduate School of Medicine at The University of Tokyo.

Overview

His research broadly focuses on aging, immune regulation, and biomedical science. He is interested in how biological systems change over time and how these changes may contribute to health, disease, and longevity.

Research Interests

Aging biology, immune aging, cellular senescence, translational research, and age-related disease mechanisms.
